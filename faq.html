Frequently asked questions

Questions about our content.

Are your stories true?

We focus on documented historical events and evidence-grounded mysteries. When claims are debated, we aim to state that uncertainty clearly.

Do you use artificial intelligence?

Yes. AI-assisted tools may support drafting, narration, imagery, editing, and workflow management. Final publication remains subject to human review.

How do you verify information?

We compare claims against available source material and revise or remove claims that are unsupported or overstated.

Why are some details described as “reported” or “disputed”?

Those terms indicate that available sources are incomplete, conflicting, or based on interpretation rather than universally accepted fact.

Can I suggest a topic?

Yes. Use the contact page and include the topic, why it matters, and any useful source links.

How can I report an error?

Email us with the video title, the specific claim, and supporting sources. We review substantive correction requests.
