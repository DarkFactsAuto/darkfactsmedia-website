About us

Stories deserve context.

Dark Facts Media is an independent digital media project focused on documented historical events, overlooked stories, and strange but verifiable moments from the past.

Our purpose

We create short-form educational media that helps viewers discover history they may not encounter in traditional summaries. Our aim is not to sensationalize tragedy, but to present compelling stories with care, context, and clear distinctions between established fact and historical debate.

What we cover

Forgotten historyHistorical disastersScientific historyArchaeologyCold War historyStrange incidentsUnsolved historical questions

How technology is used

We use software and artificial-intelligence tools to assist with drafting, narration, visual production, editing, and workflow management. These tools do not replace editorial judgment. Final content is reviewed before publication.

Corrections

Historical research can evolve. When a meaningful error is identified, we review the evidence and correct or clarify content when appropriate.
